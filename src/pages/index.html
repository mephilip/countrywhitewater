ABOUT OUR PARTNERSHIP

NATURALLY COMPATIBLE

Founded by Brent Ridge and Josh Kilmer-Purcell (more commonly known as the Beekman Boys), Beekman 1802 is one of today’s hottest lifestyle brands. With an authentic style all its own – and an appreciation for service and quality that it shares with Country Inns & Suites – Beekman 1802 makes a perfect partner.

Everything Beekman 1802 offers – from its skincare products to artisanal foods to home décor items – is made with care and designed to be enjoyed in the moment. It’s all about helping people take time to find a little bit of luxury in every day. Now make that at ever stay.

THE WHITE WATER COLLECTION

Together, Country Inns & Suites and Beekman 1802 are excited to bring you the exclusive White Water Collection bath amenity line – inspired by the world-famous mineral springs that runs behind the Beekman Mercantile in Sharon Springs, New York.

Everyone deserves a moment to relax and unwind when traveling. And whether it’s your way to start the morning or end the evening as our guest, the White Water Collection is our way to say go ahead, have your moment. Indulge. Enjoy a little luxury in every stay.
